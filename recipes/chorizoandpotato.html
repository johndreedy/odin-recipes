Chorizo and Potato Recipe

Description

Chorizo and potato is a tasty Spanish main course served with onions and garlic in a sherry sauce.

It uses new potatos cut down the middle which absorb the juices from the chorizo, lending to the overall flavour.

Other spices can be added to enhance the dish, and the amount of water added varies based on how strong you would like the sauce to be.

Ingredients

  • A chorizo sausage
  • 500g of potatos
  • A large onion
  • A clove of garlic
  • 250ml of sherry
  • Two bay leaves
  • A dash of olive oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. With a large stock pot on the hob at low heat, add a dash of olive oil.
  2. Peel and finely chop the onion, and add it to the pot.
  3. Use a garlic press to crush the garlic and add all of it to the pot.
  4. Wash the new potatos thoroughly in a sive, and chop each of them in two before adding them.
  5. Chop the chorizo into smaller pieces and add it to the mix.
  6. Now add the 250ml of sherry.
  7. If the sherry doesn't quite cover what's inside the pot, add a little extra water to dilute the sauce.
  8. Add two bay leaves.
  9. Wait until the sauce starts to boil, then put the lid on the pot and leave for 10 minutes while the oven pre-heats.
  10. Once it has cooked on the hob for 10 mins, put it in the oven with the lid off and cook for 35 mins.
  11. Take the pot out of the oven, serve and enjoy!
