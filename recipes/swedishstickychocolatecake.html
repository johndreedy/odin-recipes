Swedish Sticky Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

Swedish sticky chocolate cake, also known as Kladdkaka, is a devilishly more-ish desert straight from the Nordic country itself.

It is best served warm, similar to a brownie, with strawberries and ice cream.

Especially recommended is Swedish glace ice cream which compliments the cake well with its extra creamy flavour.

Ingredients

  • 250g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 30 cocoa powder
  • 60g of flour
  • 100g of butter
  • A tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • Some powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200C
  2. In a large bowl whisk sugar and eggs together until there is no separation
  3. Add cocoa powder and stir well with spoon
  4. Add the butter and vanilla extract and stir until there are no lumps of butter
  5. Grease a baking tray with a little more butter and pour mixture in, smoothing it out
  6. Bake for 20 mins until the top has hardened but the centre is soft
  7. Coat with powdered sugar, divide cake up and enjoy!
